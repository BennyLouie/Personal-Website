Projects

Simple Pokemon Battles

A simplified Pokemon Battle game. Users select from a list of the 151 original first-generation Pokemon and add them to their team. Each Pokemon will have a level and stats which they can update using available stat points. Users may select a Pokemon on their team to battle a random opponent. During battle, the user may choose either to attack or defend. Victories and losses are recorded on the user's battle record.

Pokemon Instagram

An app designed to very simply mimic an instagram for Pokemon. Instead of user accounts, users may select from a dropdown list to choose which Pokemon's account to view. Once selected, users may create a new post for the selected Pokemon. Users can also see all past posts for the selected Pokemon as well as delete or like the posts. Each pokemon will have an additional dropdown menu of the Pokemon it currently follows. Users may choose to like the posts of the Pokemon selected from that menu. There is currently no way to choose a Pokemon to follow.
